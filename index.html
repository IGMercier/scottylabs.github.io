What is ScottyLabs?

ScottyLabs is a student-run, interdisciplinary organization at Carnegie Mellon University dedicated to removing barriers between students’ ideas and their implementation.

Upcoming Events

  1. January 25-26

    TartanHacks 2013

    TartanHacks is a hackathon designed for people who have great ideas for cool things. Last year, TartanHacks became the biggest hackathon on CMU’s campus (and the only student-run one). We had an awesome time last year (see photos from last year and read about last year's winning hacks

    Registration will open in the first week of the Spring 2013 semester. Leading up to the hackathon, we’ll be hosting student-led, skill-building talks. At the hackathon, we’ll have expert mentors, great food, and, well, fun. More details will be available at tartanhacks.com leading up to the hackathon.

  2. December 1stPostponed Until Spring

    Demo Day

    Been working on a neat project in your free time? Want to see what other CMU students are working on in between classes? Join us for Demo Day - a place to show off your latest app or tech project. The only requirement is that you didn't make it for class.

    Presenters will each have 3 minutes to show off their project!

    Remember to be proud of your work, no matter how big or small.

What do we do?

APIS@CMU

You need data about classes for your app?
We can hook you up.

ScottyLabs is creating APIs for student data at CMU, because scraping HTML and parsing PDFs is no fun.

Go to the APIS@CMU Website

SkillSwap

I'll teach you color theory if you teach me graph theory.

Come learn from other students at SkillSwap. There are workshops about everything from programming to pitching an idea. (Some workshops: Design Process, Pitching, What is a webapp?, API Design, HTML/CSS, iOS)

This year, SkillSwap Weekend was September 8th and 9th. You can see summaries and get slides from the talks on the site.

TartanHacks

Twenty-four hours to make something awesome.

Tartanhacks is an event which brings people together to see who can build the coolest application in 24 hours.

See the TartanHacks 1 site.
