What is ScottyLabs?

ScottyLabs is a student-run, interdisciplinary organization at Carnegie Mellon University dedicated to removing barriers between students’ ideas and their implementation.

Upcoming Events

  1. Every Saturday

    Make CMU

    Come to the Studio for Creative Inquiry in the College of Fine Arts (CFA) building every saturday for meetings of Make CMU, Carneige Mellon's weekly Hacker Space. Come surround yourself with awesome people and work on whatever strikes your fancy.

What do we do?

APIS@CMU

You need data about classes for your app?
We can hook you up.

ScottyLabs is creating APIs for student data at CMU, because scraping HTML and parsing PDFs is no fun.

Go to the APIS@CMU Website

Print@ScottyLabs

Mobile printing for Carnegie Mellon

If you need to print something to the CMU Printers, send it as a PDF or plain text document to print@scottylabs.org. For more information, check out the Print@ScottyLabs Website.

TartanHacks

Twenty-four hours to make something awesome.

Tartanhacks is an event which brings people together to see who can build the coolest application in 24 hours. TartanHacks 2014, the third annual event, will take place from January 31 to February 1, 2014.

Check us out at tartanhacks.com.
