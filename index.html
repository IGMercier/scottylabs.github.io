What is ScottyLabs?

ScottyLabs is a student organization at Carnegie Mellon University. We use our technical expertise to promote innovation in practical ways.

What do we do?

APIS@CMU

You need data about classes for your app?
We can hook you up.

ScottyLabs is creating APIs for student data at CMU, because scraping HTML and parsing PDFs is no fun.

TartanHacks

Twenty-four hours to make something awesome.

Tartanhacks is an event which brings people together to see who can build the coolest application in 24 hours.
