Projects

  • APIs@CMU

    You need data about classes for your app?
    We can hook you up.

    ScottyLabs's flagship project, APIs@CMU gives student and faculty developers access to university data like the student directory and schedule of classes in an easily-parseable JSON format.

    The APIs@CMU project is planned to launch in the Fall 2012 semester. Visit apis.scottylabs.org for more information and to see documentation for the APIs.

  • TartanHacks

    Twenty-four hours to make something awesome.

    A hackathon is an event where a bunch of people get together in teams for a fixed amount of time to make something cool. Prizes, pizza, and caffeine are usually involved. TartanHacks is a hackathon designed for people who have great ideas for cool things. At TartanHacks, ScottyLabs will help you turn your idea into reality.

    We provide seminars on web and mobile development, expert mentors available for the full 24 hours, and food that's way better than pizza. TartanHacks happens each year at the beginning of the spring semester; go to tartanhacks.com for more information.

  • Demo Days

    It's time to show off.

    CMU students do a lot of really cool things in clubs, or just in their free time, but we rarely get a chance to show off. Come to our Demo Days— twice each semester— to show off what you've done or to see what your fellow students have done. All kinds of projects are welcome, not just technical ones!

  • SkillSwap

    I'll teach you color theory if you teach me graph theory.

    SkillSwap Weekend is a two day event during which expert student volunteers will teach other students new skills. There are workshops about everything from programming to pitching an idea. Our workshops are targeted at different skill levels, so that any student can find something to learn.

    Drop by any time for a one-hour workshop and get free food and swag! The complete schedule's coming soon, but some of our workshops include:

    • Design Process
    • Pitching
    • What is a Webapp?
    • API Design
    • HTML/CSS
    • iOS

  • Start-Up Tour Pittsburgh

    Entrepreneurship in the Steel City.

    Want to learn more about start-ups? You'll get your chance at Start-Up Tour Pittsburgh you'll get to visit companies, meet CMU alums who have started companies, and network with local entrepreneurs.

    On November 1st, ScottyLabs and Project Olympus took 25 students on a tour of several of the startups in Pittsburgh. Starting at Project Olympus on Craig St, students made their way to AlphaLabs, Schell Games, 4Moms. The day ended with dinner with Luis von Ahn at DuoLingo. Through presentations, Q&A panels, and building tours, participants received a glimpse of life at a startup. It introduced them to the challenges and benefits of working at one. In addition, students received valuable advice about how to start their own. We hope that next time we can make it bigger and better!
