Backstory

How we came to exist

In the summer of 2011, Vinay Vemuri and Amy Quispe had a conversation. Vinay was working on a project that involved scraping tons of data— by hand— from Carnegie Mellon's websites to make that information useful for programmers. Together, they launched the project that would become APIs@CMU, an effort to compile all of the university's publicly available data into a machine-readable format and, with the cooperation of the administration, provide that data to students and faculty. Over the course of the fall 2011 semester, the project gradually grew to include more people. In November of 2011, the group decided that it had grown big enough that it needed a name. Thus, ScottyLabs was born.
