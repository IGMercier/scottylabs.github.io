Partners

Carnegie Mellon School of Computer Science

The Carnegie Mellon School of Computer Science has provided space for ScottyLabs to host events in the beautiful Gates-Hillman Building. SCS has also provided administrative, organizational, and logistic help for our events; TartanHacks would not be possible without their help. SCS is also the home school for many ScottyLabs members Find out more at www.cs.cmu.edu.

Project Olympus

Project Olympus provides advice, resources, and connections for Carnegie Mellon students and faculty who want to start companies. Olympus provides resources for ScottyLabs projects that want to explore their commercial potential, and also provides organizational help with ScottyLabs events.

PennApps

The good people of Penn Apps helped to inspire TartanHacks, and when we decided to actually go through with running a hackathon, they gave us advice for how to organize everything.
