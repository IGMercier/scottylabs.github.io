Donate

Let's build something together!

ScottyLabs powers innovation across the Carnegie Mellon campus, but we can't do it alone. Join us by helping fund our projects and events. We run everything from TartanHacks to API's@CMU. Learn more about our projects here.

How to Donate

ScottyLabs donations can be made electronically using CMU's online form.

Just follow these three steps:

  1. 1. Select "Give to area(s) of your choice"
  2. 2. Select "Other" in the pop-up box
  3. 3. Fill in "ScottyLabs" in the Other box

Alternatively, you are welcome to donate to ScottyLabs directly using cash or a check (please make checks payable to Carnegie Mellon University, and write "ScottyLabs" in the note box), and give them to one of our team members.
